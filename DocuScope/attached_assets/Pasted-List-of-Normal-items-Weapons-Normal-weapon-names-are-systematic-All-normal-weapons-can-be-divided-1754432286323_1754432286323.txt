List of Normal items
Weapons
Normal weapon names are systematic. All normal weapons can be divided into four series:

Weapons with levels 1-25 have no prefix
Weapons with levels 28-55 are prefixed "Flawless"
Weapons with levels 56-76 are prefixed "Pure"
Weapons with levels 78-98 are prefixed "Impeccable"
Within each of those divisions, weapons of various materials are found, with certain materials being of higher level than others. For example, the Oak (sometimes Oak Wood) weapons are the lowest-leveled in each series, while the Light Jungle weapons are the highest-leveled.

Note that some weapons may not have every expected weapon - for example, there is no Light Jungle Stick.
